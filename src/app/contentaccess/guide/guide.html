Guide to Troubleshooting Access Issues

 If you are able to view some pages (e.g. the home page) but not others, please try the following steps:

  1. View our subscriber list to check whether your institution has a subscription.

  2. If so, try accessing the site from on campus, or follow your library's instructions for using your institution's proxy server for viewing library resources from off campus. Some institutions may require you to use a proxy server even while on campus.

    If these methods do not work, please email us at info@phoenixbioinformatics.org and provide the name of your institution and your IP address from an on-campus location. To find your IP address, please use http://ip-lookup.net/.

  3. If your institution is not listed as a subscriber, please contact your librarian and request a subscription. You can also contact us for help in setting up a subscription for your university or company .

  4. If you need immediate access, consider subscribing as an individual while the library subscription is in process.

If you are not able to view any pages, or if any page gives an error message, please email us at info@phoenixbioinformatics.org reporting the error message or providing a screen shot, and giving the sequence of actions that you took to produce the error.
